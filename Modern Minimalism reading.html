Modern Minimalism reading response

Stand-out features of a modern website are;

  • Flexible grid layout
  • High-quality product images
  • Muted palette

A modern web design allows the viewer to achieve their goal in an efficient manner.
With this, the website has minimal design elements, the viewer then doesn't get distracted by flashy or unimportant information that is being shown on their screen.

Modern minimalism websites have grown more and more over the years and seems to be more commonly used now.

The focus on these websites isn't for the look- emphasis on what the content of the page has, because the content is the most important.

The benefits with minimal design elements for the website, can develop a faster loading time for different screens. As well viewers can focus more on the Call to Action aspects of the page, they will be more likely to interact with the page rather than click off the page.

Finally, these sites are more aimed to be for the future. They can age well overtime and usually cannot go out of style.
